How FoodLink Works

1. Donors Post Surplus Food

Restaurants, hotels, and grocery stores list their surplus food with details like type, quantity, and pickup time.

2. NGOs Get Notified

Nearby NGOs and food banks receive notifications about available donations in their area.

3. Quick Pickup & Distribution

NGOs schedule pickups and distribute the food to people in need through their networks.

4. Track Impact

Both donors and NGOs can track how much food was saved and how many people were fed.
